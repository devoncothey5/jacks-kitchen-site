Services

Jack’s Kitchen offers a range of private chef services with a modern touch. Choose from intimate Winery Pairing Dinners, gourmet At-Home Dinners, or convenient Meal Prep plans – all fully customizable to your needs and preferences.

Winery Pairings

Elevate your winery experience with multi-course dinners expertly paired with Walla Walla wines. We collaborate with local winemakers to design seasonal menus that complement each pour. Enjoy an unforgettable evening of food and wine in a vineyard or tasting room setting.

At-Home Dinners

Dine restaurant-style in the comfort of your home. Ideal for 2–12 guests, these at-home dinners feature custom menus crafted to your occasion. Chef Jack handles everything – on-site cooking, elegant plating, and even cleanup – so you can relax and enjoy a memorable meal with your guests.

Meal Prep

Simplify your weeknights with our personalized meal prep service. We create weekly menus tailored to your dietary preferences and schedule. Each meal is prepared from scratch, portioned, and packaged with reheating instructions. Enjoy healthy, delicious dishes at home without the hassle of cooking daily.
